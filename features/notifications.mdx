## Purpose

Notifications provide a global inbox for asynchronous events that happen outside the active turn.

## Common notification types

- `job_complete`
- `job_error`
- `tab_complete`

## Delivery model

- Notifications are persisted in SQLite.
- They are broadcast to the renderer through WebSocket events.
- The frontend can combine persisted counts with live events to keep badge state accurate.

WebSocket events:

- `notification_added`
- `notification_dismissed`
- `notifications_cleared`

## Relevant endpoints

- `GET /api/notifications`
- `GET /api/notifications/count`
- `DELETE /api/notifications/{notification_id}`
- `DELETE /api/notifications`
